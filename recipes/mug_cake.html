Mug Cake!

Back to Hub

Ingredients

  • Mug
  • flour
  • butter
  • chocolate
  • sugar
  • vanilla

Steps

  1. mix flour, sugar and salt into Mug
  2. mix chocolate, butter and vanilla together in a separate bowl
  3. mix chocolatey butter into mug
  4. Give it goooooood spin!!
  5. microwave for however long you feel like!

    6. IMPORTANT

  6. Let cool for 10 mins or you'll die!!!!!
