Spicy Popcorn

Back to Hub

  • Corn Kernals
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red chili oil
  • Coconut oil/another high smoke point oil
  • Garlic
  1. Veeeery lightly brown the garlic over low heat with the oil of choice
  2. Pour garlic oil slurry into small bowl and add desired amount of spicy chili to it.
  3. Cover bottom of large pot with oil
  4. Set stovetop to high and wait until the oil shimmers and shines
  5. Add juuuuust enough kernals to cover the bottom of the pot. No more! Else, they'll burn!
  6. Wait for kernals to cease popping. (~2-3 seconds between pops)
  7. Pour spicy garlic slurry over the pot and mix vigorously.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
